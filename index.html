What is ugliness to people?

We are interested in finding out what constitutes ugliness for people. One common assumption about ugliness is that it is merely the opposite of aesthetically pleasingness (aka beauty). However, we believe that there is more depth and dimension to this quality.



In this study, you have rated your impression on different images regarding various adjectives. By measuring your impression regarding several adjectives with negative meaning, we can look for relationships between ugliness and these other adjectives. For example, if the adjective “boring” is highly correlated with ugliness, then we can speculate that an image being ugly entails it being boring to some degree.
